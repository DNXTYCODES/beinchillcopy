Bentsi-Enchill, Letsa & Ankomah Wins Chambers Africa Awards Ghana Law Firm of the Year, 2024

February 26, 2024

Chambers and Partners has named Bentsi-Enchill, Letsa & Ankomah as the Ghana Law Firm of the Year in the Chambers Global Africa Awards, 2024.

The award culminates a year of working with clients on some of the market's most significant and innovative transactions, projects and disputes.

“We are grateful to our clients for their continued trust in us and to our diligent team of lawyers and business professionals for their commitment towards delivering excellence. The award is a testament to our enduring tradition of providing cutting edge solutions to our clients.” said Managing Partner, Seth Asante.

The Chambers Africa awards “recognise a law firm’s pre-eminence in key jurisdictions in the region. They also reflect achievements over the past 12 months including outstanding work, impressive strategic growth and excellence in client service."
